About Us

The year was 2001, and our founders kicked off their telecom career together by creating a new disruptive industry called Hosted PBX. One provider had great phones. Another had great software. Some worked well across locations, some worked better at a single location. All required a third party to install and service each component separately.

In the end, the business customer was mostly frustrated by poor service, products that never quite solved the problem, and solutions that cost far too much money to buy and maintain.

Though the years the team took on the established telecom industry. The fight was a good fight and others caught on to the beauty of the model. Create a happy customer by charging a fair price, focusing on delivering a great customer experience, and building a fantastic product!

The original idea behind ERATECycle was to leverage the E-Rate experience gained during those years in the Cloud/Hosted PBX world with a deep understanding of how to shift ineffective service relationships into product relationships.

ERATECycle exists to provide instant, highly transparent and much improved guidance to Eligible Entities for managing the E-Rate process.

So here is the vision:

  • Instant understanding of existing service
  • Clear and complete planning tools
  • Transparent cycle management
  • Instant and complete filing tools

Our goal is to position each Eligible Entity in control of the E-Rate process. Remove the mystery behind E-Rate and USAC, and make it simple to successfully manage the entire E-Rate process.
