Track E-Rate Funding

ERATECycle was created to help E-Rate applicants and service providers track E-RATE funding, meet critical deadlines, and make participating in the E-rate program simple. ERATECycle incorporates all new regulatory guidance and clarifications into new reports, features and tools to assist filing schools and libraries with the process.

Each year E-RATE eligible entities lose out on millions of dollars because of deficiencies in managing the E-RATE process. ERATECycle helps entities make the most of their eligible E-RATE funding with many simple tools and features.

Immediate ERATECycle helps eligible entities consistently file fully compliant applications. All customer data is stored and archived in our highly available, secure and redundant cloud. Much of the filing information for an entity must be available for potential review for up to 10 years, ERATECycle makes this not only possible, but simple too.
